Bulldogs Weather Radio (WXB26) is a specialized weather broadcast service operating in cooperation with the National Weather Service. We utilize an advanced BMH (Broadcast Message Handler) emulation core to deliver 24/7 weather information, marine forecasts, and emergency alerting.

Our mission is to provide life-saving weather data with professional-grade clarity and 100% SAME/EAS compliance. This platform is part of the Weather-Ready Nation (WRN) Ambassador program.
